ANNIVERSARY OF 65 YEARS OF TRAINING, 55 YEARS OF ESTABLISHMENT OF HANOI UNIVERSITY OF CIVIL ENGINEERING

On November 19, 2021, Hanoi University of Civil Engineering (HUCE) solemnly held a ceremony to celebrate the Anniversary of 65 years of training, 55 years of establishment (1956-1966-2021), to announce the Decision on changing the name of the University, and to receive the Emulation Flag awarded by the Government.

  • OPENING CEREMONY OF THE 18TH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF AUF

    From September 21 to 23, 2021, the Opening Ceremony of the 18th General Assembly of AUF (Agence Universitaire de la Francophonie) was held at Politehnica University (Bucharest, Romania) to elect the President and members of the Board of Directors of AUF for the 2021-2025 term. On behalf of Hanoi University of Civil Engineering, as an official member of AUF, Assoc. Prof. Pham Duy Hoa, Rector, represented the University in the General Assembly, and voted for the presidency and for electing the members of the AUF Board.
