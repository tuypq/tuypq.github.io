MOC signed between British Council and Ministry of Education and Training

On 31 October 2021, in Edinburgh, witnessed by Viet Nam’s Prime Minister Pham Minh Chinh, Vice Minister of Education and Training, Assoc. Prof Nguyen Van Phuc and Mr Charlie Walker, Director Global Network of the British Council, signed a renewed Memorandum of Collaboration (MOC) between the British Council and the Ministry of Education and Training. The MOC will further British Council’s long-term engagement with the Ministry to support Viet Nam’s effort in their internationalisation of education through our work in higher education leadership and development, academic and student mobility, science, and research, English language, among other areas.

TNE E-book – Studying for a UK Qualification in East Asia

Do you want to get an UK degree but you have commitments that prevent you to study in the UK?
With the Transnational Education Programme (TNE), you can study easily and flexibly in your home country and still get the same qualification as the students studying in the UK.

Hungry Water

Hungry Water is a climate action project that develops project-based learning on the impact of erosion and landslides along the Mekong river for high school students in Dong Thap province. This is one of the ten climate action projects under the Rivers of Life initiative that highlights the impact of climate challenges on the Mekong River. See more Hungry Water, and other climate action projects which are implemented by youth leaders and high school students through the lens of science, arts, communications and education, and which were featured at the British Council’s pavilion at COP26 in Glasgow.
